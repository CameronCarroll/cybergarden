Exploring Cybernetics

Welcome to the Cybernetics Digital Garden, a place to explore the rich interdisciplinary field of cybernetics and its connections to modern artificial intelligence, systems theory, and control systems.

Cybernetics, coined by Norbert Wiener in the 1940s, is the study of regulatory systems, their structures, constraints, and possibilities. This digital garden is designed to provide an accessible entry point to cybernetic concepts, history, and applications.
